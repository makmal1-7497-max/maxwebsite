A reaction-based game built from the ground up. Experience the unique fusion of the physical world (Arduino) and the digital world (PictoBlox)!

HOW TO PLAY

Objective:

Reach a score of **100** before the time runs out!

Gain Points:

Click The Peace And Justice. GREEN LIGHT OR WHITE LIGHT will pop up from Aduino Uno Your score will increase by **+1**.

Avoid Penalties:

If player missclick, you will lose **-1** points and RED LIGHT will pop out from Aduino Uno.

Game Over:

The game ends when you reach the target score or when your score drops to zero.
